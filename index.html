Modern Family: The Pritchetts!

A brief guide into the Modern Family world

Claire, Jay, and Mitch

Jay Pritchett

The head of the Pritchett family is Jason Francis "Jay" Pritchett. Joe, Mitchell, and Claire are his children. He is Manny's stepfather and is wed to Gloria. In addition to being the creator and CEO of Dog Beds By Stella, he is a Vietnam War veteran and the previous CEO of Pritchett's Closets & Blinds. Jay is portrayed as a military veteran who is highly severe, gruff, and manipulative in several ways. Nevertheless, he is a good man who worries for his family's well-being in spite of everything. He has the ability to connect with others, and when one of his girlfriends breaks up with him, he gives Manny a shoulder to cry on.

Claire Pritchett

Claire Melinda Pritchett or Claire Dunphy is a typical housewife, married to Phill Dunphy, and a mom of three: Hailey Dunphy, Alex Dunphy, and Luke Dunphy. Is known as the OCD freak and is the one who keeps the family organized and intact. Bored of her repetitive life, she decided to work for her dad's (Jay Pritchett) company, Pritchett's Closets & Blinds. Not long after, when Jay entered his retirement years, the company was given to her.

    Mitchell Pritchett

    Mitchell Vincent Pritchett is the secondborn of Dede Pritchett and Jay Pritchett and the husband of Cameron Tucker. He adopted his daughter, Lily Tucker-Pritchett, from Vietnam and works as a lawyer. Among the Pritchetts, he maintains the closest relationship with his birth mother, Dede Pritchett, and has always helped her keep in touch with the rest of the family. Mitchell is known for his sarcasm and eye rolls. Regardless, his ability to remain realistic, objective, yet loving is what makes him a great husband, father, and son to the family.
